Queso

Serves: 8   Prep Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound processed cheese (Velveeta or similar)
  • 10oz can diced tomatoes and green chilis - undrained (Rotel or similar)

Instructions

  1. Melt the cheese in a large glass bowl in the microwave. (Alternatively, you may melt the cheese in a slow cooker or in a pot on the stove. I prefer the microwave because it is more efficient.)
  2. Add the diced tomatoes and green chilis.
  3. Stir until an even consitency is attained.

Suggested serving

  • Tortilla chips
  • Enchiladas
  • Beans and rice
  • For extra protein, add ground beef to the mixture. Make sure the beef is fully cooked before adding it to the cheese.

Guacamole

Serves: 4   Prep Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 clove of fresh crushed garlic
  • Juice of 1/4 lemon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Instructions

  1. Remove the peel and seeds from the avocadoes.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, add the avocadoes, crushed garlic, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  3. Mash all the ingredients together with a large fork or potato masher until an even consistency is attained.

Suggested serving

  • Tortilla chips, sour cream, and your favorite salsa
  • Enchiladas
  • Quesadillas

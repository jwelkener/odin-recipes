        <h1>Grilled Salmon Chelsea</h1>

        <img src="https://github.com/jwelkener/recipes/blob/main/Salmon%20Chelsea.png" alt="Salmon Chelsea" width="200">

        <h4>Serves: 2 &emsp; Prep Time: 15 minutes &emsp; Cook Time: 20 minutes</h4>

        <div>

                <h3>Ingredients</h3>

                <ul>

                        <li>2 Salmon fillets</li>

                        <li>4 oz Brie cheese, sliced</li>

                        <li>1/2 cup Cranberry sauce</li>

                        <li>Fresh thyme leaves</li>

                        <li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>

                </ul>

        </div>

        <div>

                <h3>Instructions</h3>

                <ol>

                        <li>Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.</li>

                        <li>Cut two sheets of aluminum foil, each large enough to wrap a salmon fillet.</li>

                        <li>Place a salmon fillet on each sheet of foil.</li>

                        <li>Season the salmon with salt and pepper.</li>

                        <li>Top each salmon fillet with slices of Brie cheese.</li>

                        <li>Spoon cranberry sauce over the Brie cheese.</li>

                        <li>Sprinkle fresh thyme leaves over the top.</li>

                        <li>Create foil "boats" by folding up the edges of the foil around the salmon, creating a sealed packet.</li>

                        <li>Place the foil packets on the grill and cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.</li>

                </ol>

        </div>

        <div>

                <h3>Suggested serving</h3>

                <ul>

                        <li>Serve the grilled Salmon Chelsea with a side salad and crusty bread.</li>

                        <li>Pair with a glass of white wine or sparkling water with lemon.</li>

                </ul>

                <h3>Tips</h3>

                <ul>

                        <li>Feel free to customize the toppings with your favorite herbs or additional ingredients.</li>

                        <li>Check the salmon for doneness by gently poking it with a fork. It should easily flake apart when fully cooked.</li>

                </ul>

        </div>

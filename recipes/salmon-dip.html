Smoked Salmon Dip

Serves: 4   Prep Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Smoked Salmon
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2-3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Fresh dill and/or chives (optional)
  • Tabasco sauce(optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix salmon, sour cream, and mayo in a food processor. (If you don't have a food processor, you can mix and mash with a large fork.)
  2. For extra kick, add dill, chives, Tabasco sauce, or similar.

Suggested serving

  • Crackers (Saltine, Ritz, Triscuit, or similar)
  • Gluten-free crackers
  • Toast or bagels
  • Dip with veggies: baby carrots, cucumber slices, bell pepper slices, or similar

Tips

  • Most smoked salmon is already very salty, and mayo is also quite salty, so unless all your ingredients are unsalted I would not recommend adding any salt.
  • If your salmon is too salty, you can add more sour cream to take some of the edge off.
